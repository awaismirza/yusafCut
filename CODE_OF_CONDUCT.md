# Code of Conduct

YusafCut adopts the [Contributor Covenant 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

In short:

- Be respectful, inclusive, and patient.
- Focus on technical merits; assume good faith.
- Harassment, discrimination, and personal attacks are not tolerated.

Report violations privately to the maintainers via the email address listed in
`SECURITY.md`. All reports are confidential.

Enforcement decisions rest with the maintainers. We will explain our reasoning
when we act, and we will not act on hearsay.
